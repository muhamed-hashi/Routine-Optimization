Project Proposal

Problem Description

Overview

We aim to create a website that assists users in tracking habits, with the ultimate goal of fostering healthier routines and habits. In a time when distractions are ubiquitous, even a simple notification on your phone can lead to endless hours of scrolling or media consumption. As noted, “Our smartphones, the smartest 21st-century innovation in humanity, can be the biggest distraction of the present age.” [1] By building intuitive resources, such as a website for habit tracking, we offer users a space to monitor their needs. This can help users take accountability for their actions, build satisfying routines, reduce distractions, and overall improve their everyday lives.

Information Problems

A key information technology issue in this space involves the personalization of data relating to the user. Habit tracking is not a one-size-fits-all solution. Another issue is ensuring accountability in habit-building applications. To effectively support users in building habits, the application needs to be user-friendly and incorporate features that keep users consistently engaged. The challenge lies in balancing ease of use with the necessary functionality to support diverse user needs.

Other Attempts

There are many examples of productivity-assisting resources available. One notable example is Habitica, “a free habit-building and productivity app that treats your real life like a game” [2]. Habitica uses gamification, incorporating rewards and punishments, to motivate users to build and maintain healthy habits. This approach adds an element of fun and engagement to the process of habit tracking, making it a unique and interesting solution in the space of productivity apps.

App Description

Who Are The Users?

We expect this resource to assist a wide range of individuals who are looking to organize their day-to-day lives. This includes students, employed individuals, parents, and anyone who aims to build routines and structure in their daily activities. This type of resource is inclusive and designed to be useful for any demographic wishing to improve their productivity and habit formation.

What Are The Features?

  • Goal setting
  • Daily habit tracking
  • Interactive progress visualization
  • Tips and insights on habit formation
  • Search and filter goals

Functionality Walkthrough

Our app will include the following interactive features:

  • Setting and Managing Goals: Users can create, edit, and delete goals, set deadlines, and track progress. Users can categorize and organize their goals.
  • Daily Habit Tracking: Users can log their daily habits, receive notifications, and view logs. The app will include a demo mode for testing.
  • Interactive Progress Visualization: Users can interact with progress charts and graphs, filter data, and customize their dashboards. Users can click on data points for detailed analysis.
  • Search and Filter Goals: Users can search and filter their goals based on various criteria, and organize goals in different views.

What Kind Of Information Is Viewed?

Users will primarily view their own habit tracking data, including progress over time, upcoming tasks, and achievement milestones. Additionally, the app may provide insights and tips on habit formation based on aggregated user data.

How Will This App Solve The Problem?

By providing a structured environment for users to track their habits, this app helps reduce distractions and improve focus. The notifications and progress tracking features help keep users accountable, while the insights and tips support them in building and maintaining healthy habits.
